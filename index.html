Christian Blood Sausage

A Christian Blood Sausage is a sausage filled with the blood of slaughtered Christians (usually monks) that is cooked or dried and mixed with a filler until it is thick enough to solidify when cooled.

Mead

Mead, also called hydromel, is an alcoholic beverage made by fermenting honey mixed with water, and sometimes with added ingredients such as fruits, spices, grains, or hops. The alcoholic content ranges from about 3.5% ABV to more than 20%. Wikipedia

Roasted Hog

A pig roast or hog roast is an event or gathering which involves the barbecuing of a whole pig. Pig roasts, under a variety of names, are a common traditional celebration event in many places including the Philippines, Puerto Rico and Cuba.
